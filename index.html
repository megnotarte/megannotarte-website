About

I help people and organizations find their footing in a world being reshaped by AI.

In late 2023, I co-founded AI Portland, now one of the Pacific Northwest's most active AI communities, with over 2,500 members. Through monthly events, panels, workshops, and a podcast, we create space for honest, accessible conversation about how generative AI is changing work and life. I've been a featured speaker and panelist on AI literacy in the Portland Business Journal and beyond.

Additionally, I spent nearly two decades building things: first as a developer, then as Senior Project Manager, and ultimately as Partner and VP at Cloud Four, a boutique web agency serving enterprise clients like Walmart and Old Navy. I've run hiring, finances, culture, and operations. I've built teams and I've made payroll.

What I'm drawn to now: roles where leadership, community, and technology intersect: Executive Director, Director of AI Community or Developer Relations, or Head of AI Literacy. Places where building something real is the whole job.
